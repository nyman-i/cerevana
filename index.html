RRT

Read premises like “A is north-east of B”, hold the relations in mind, and judge whether the conclusion follows. Distinction, syllogisms, spatial reasoning in 2D/3D/4D, and more — against the clock. Based on Syllogimous by 4skinSkywalker, continued by ikokusovereignty and soamsy.

Trains: fluid reasoning — relational integration, logical inference and mental-model building under time pressure.

N-BACK

Watch squares light up on a grid while letters are spoken. Signal whenever a position or sound repeats from N trials back — the classic dual n-back protocol from Paul Hoskinson's Brain Workshop. The level adapts to your performance.

Trains: working memory — continuous updating, attention control and interference resolution.
