How to play

Digits from 1–9 play one at a time. For each new digit, answer with the result of it and the digit right before it - by default the sum, so hearing "3" then "5" means you type 8.

Answer as soon as you know it - type it or click it on the answer buttons; there's no submit step, it's checked instantly. Answer correctly several times in a row and the pace speeds up; miss a few in a row and it slows back down.
