How to play

Digits from 1–9 play one at a time. For each new digit, answer with the result of it and the digit right before it — by default the sum, so hearing "3" then "5" means you type 8.

Type your answer as soon as you know it; there's no submit button, it's checked as you type. Answer correctly several times in a row and the pace speeds up; miss a few in a row and it slows back down.
