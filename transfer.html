Testing protocol - read before your first session
  • Wait 4–6 months of consistent training between your before- and after-test on any one test. Testing too soon mostly measures practice effects, not real change - a popup's delta summary flags any pair under ~120 days.
  • Test in a quiet, distraction-free environment.
  • Be rested (8+ hours of sleep), fed and hydrated. Don't test while sick or under acute stress.
  • Max 2 tests per day, to avoid mental fatigue.
  • 5–10 minutes of light warm-up is fine, but avoid intense Mindbuilding right before a test - it can cause fatigue effects.
