How to play

A cube flashes in the grid while other cues play. Remember what happened N trials ago and press the matching key whenever a modality repeats: position, color, shape/image, or audio. Several can match at once.

In tally modes you instead type the count of matches each trial — self-paced, no trial timer.

The Classic, Combination and Arithmetic groups follow the Brain Workshop protocol: grid positions and spoken letters, cross-modal matches, typed arithmetic answers, and the crab, self-paced and Jaeggi variants.
